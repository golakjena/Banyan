About Us

BANYAN INVESTMENT GROUP | OUR STRENGTH LIES IN OUR ROOTS

Banyan Investment Group is an Inc. 5000 family-owned company that acquires, develops and manages premium select-service hotels in the United States. While other investment groups can compromise quality as they aggressively pursue growth, since 1976 we have leaned on conservative forecasting, decades of hands-on hospitality experience, and a clear understanding of risk to make intelligent investments that reward our investors, our team members, our hotels and our communities.
