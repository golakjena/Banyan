Acquisition, Rebrand

Aloft, 254 rooms Atlanta, Georgia
Solution
  • Acquired as a Days Inn, July 2012 for $19.5MM or $76,800 / room.
  • $20m renovation to re-brand to Aloft (Starwood Hotels), increased RevPAR by 107%.
  • Upon stabilization, asset was sold in Aug 2016 to a public REIT.
  • Realized return of 22% IRR and 2.12x equity multiple.

Ground Up New Development

Hampton Inn & Suites, 90 rooms Gulf Breeze, Florida
Solution
  • Acquired existing land parcel with improvements of $10.14MM May 2013.
  • Full entitlement, designing, sourcing, procuring and construction in a high barrier to entry market.
  • Developed into 90-room Hampton Inn & Suites hotel.
  • $2.1MM equity investment; currently yielding a 17% COC return annually.

Acquisition/Management Realignment

Acquisition, Rebrand, Management Realignment

Holiday Inn Express, 128 rooms Alpharetta, Georgia
Solution
  • Acquired as a Sierra Suites, July 2013 for $7.38MM or $59,500/room.
  • $1.76M renovation and rebranding to Holiday Inn Express in March 2014.
  • Increased RevPAR by 20% and eliminated management inefficiencies.
  • Returned 100% of initial equity invested; currently yielding a 22% COC return annually.

Acquisition, Rebrand, Management Realignment

Holiday Inn Express, 101 rooms Sarasota, Florida
Solution
  • Acquired as a Country Inn, July 2011 for $4.8MM or $47,500/room.
  • Significant Renovation / rebranding to Holiday Inn Express Jan. 2012.
  • Drove revenues 15-20% ahead of budget due to rebranding.
  • Eliminated management inefficiencies and increased top line revenue by 20%, thus increasing NOI margin.
  • Sold in July 2013 to a regional operator.
  • Realized return of 42% IRR and 1.97x equity multiple.
