Leanne Lloyd

Director of Human Resources

Leanne Lloyd, SHRM-SCP, SPHR serves as the Corporate Director of Human Resources. Responsible for employee relations and corporate policy, Leanne focuses on regulatory compliance and training as well playing an integral role in ensuring healthy and productive work environments.

Leanne began her management and HR career in the retail sector before moving to the healthcare industry where she spent 9 years as the Director of Human Resources for a non-profit behavioral healthcare facility in the Panhandle of Florida. She facilitates training sessions for management teams and other HR professionals and is asked to speak at events on topics such as compliance with labor law, leadership training, recruitment and retention, and many other human resource related issues. Leanne is certified by the Society of Human Resource Management as a Senior Certified Professional (SHRM-SCP) as well as being certified by the HR Certification Institute as a Senior Professional in Human Resources (SPHR). Leanne graduated from Northwest Florida State College with a degree in Business Administration and earned her BS degree in Business – Human Resource Management from Western Governor’s University. She currently serves as President for the Emerald Coast chapter of Society for Human Resources (SHRM) and has previously served as President for the Walton County Satellite Chapter of Emerald Coast SHRM. In addition, Leanne serves on the Board of Directors for Emerald Coast Crime Stoppers and participates in Relay for Life campaigns. In the past, she has served on the Board of Directors for Walton County Habitat for Humanity and has volunteered as a Loaned Executive for the Okaloosa-Walton United Way. Leanne is a member of National SHRM and has been a member of the Emerald Coast SHRM chapter since 2010. Leanne lives in DeFuniak Springs, FL with her son.
