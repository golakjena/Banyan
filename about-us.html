WHY SELECT SERVICE HOTELS?

Banyan is disciplined and focused on assets that fit the select service category of hotels. These brands represent major institutional flags recognized by more than half the population of the US, brands such as Hampton Inn by Hilton, Courtyard by Marriott, Holiday Inn Express by IHG, and the like. These assets have lower operational overhead, as the properties have minimal staffing models. Predictable operating expenses allow for smoother returns for our shareholders and macro lodging fundamentals remain positive for the foreseeable future.

Be Transparent and Accountable

We will always be clear in our communication and open with sharing salient information. We will strive to foster an environment of trust. Our primary endeavor is to strive for dependable and consistent performance with all of our investments.

Be Humble, Respectful and Dedicated

Through our depth of experience, thorough due diligence, and conservative underwriting and forecasting methodology, we will approach all investments with full confidence that we will be successful. We will remain humble in our endeavors, always giving praise and credit to those who helped us achieve successes. We will remain respectful to all stakeholders and expect the same from those with whom we conduct business.

We will remain steadfast, purposeful, and committed in all of our undertakings.

Embrace Opportunity

We expect all of our team members to embrace opportunity by leaning into challenges. In return, we aim to provide development and both personal and professional growth opportunities for all team members who are motivated and have ambition for improvement.

We actively seek to recognize and promote those team members who embrace opportunity.

Create Value for Shareholders

We are a value driven firm and will remain fully focused on efficiencies designed to increase investment returns. This will be achieved through continual evaluation of cost saving measures.

We have worked tirelessly to earn a stellar reputation with our Stakeholders. We will remain unwavering in fostering this standing by operating with a high level of integrity combined with an unquestionable fiduciary responsibility to our investors.

Family Matters

We recognize the paramount importance of family and commit to placing family as the number one priority while creating a healthy work-life balance.
